The Lighthouse's Secret

The old lighthouse stood sentinel on the rocky coast, its beacon dark for decades. Locals whispered about strange lights seen flickering in its tower on stormy nights. Sara, a curious historian, decided to investigate. As she climbed the winding stairs, a chill ran down her spine. In the lantern room, she found an ancient logbook, its final entry abruptly ending mid-sentence.

Suddenly, the door slammed shut behind her. The beacon sprang to life, its light pulsing in an odd pattern. Sara realized with growing unease that it wasn't signaling to ships, but to something out in the inky depths of the ocean. As shadows moved beneath the waves, Sara wondered if some mysteries were better left unsolved.
