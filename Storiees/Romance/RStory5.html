Verses of Love

Emma loved her job at the cozy bookstore on Main Street, surrounded by the scent of old pages and fresh coffee. Every day at 3 PM, a handsome stranger would come in, browse the poetry section, and leave without buying anything. One rainy afternoon, the man finally approached the counter. "I'm Alex," he said with a shy smile. "I've been trying to work up the courage to talk to you for weeks." Emma's heart fluttered. "I'm Emma. What changed today?"

Alex pulled out a rain-soaked notebook. "I've been writing you poems. I thought it was time to share them." As Emma read his heartfelt words, she realized sometimes the best love stories aren't just found in books - they can unfold right before your eyes. Their eyes met, and in that moment, both knew this was just the beginning of their own romantic tale.

Emma's cheeks flushed as she finished reading Alex's poems. "These are beautiful," she said softly, looking up at him. Alex rubbed the back of his neck nervously.
